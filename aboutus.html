The average Striped Bass anglers catch on Beaver Lake are 20 to 25lbs. But what makes Beaver Lake stand out from other striped bass fisheries is the opprotunity to capture a trophy Striper over 50lbs and even having a shot at stiper pushing triple digits. The state record is a 64-pound Striper. As such, our goal is to make one of our anglers the next record holder with a striper over 80lbs. You can also enjoy World class Fishing on the White River in Arkansas. Come join our Experienced Bass Fishing Guides for a day or more of unforgettable Bass Fishing. Fisherman from around the country come to the White River in Arkansas to enjoy world class fishing in clean, cold water. This is some of the best fishing water on the White River and some of the most scenic views in all of Arkansas. Large mouth, small mouth, striper, white, spotted, rock, Ozark, shadow, and many more bass species are all caught here. Arkansas has produced stiper bass up to 68lbs 4oz., with numerous others recorded in the fifty pound class. However it is not only the size, but also the variety of bass species that lure anglers from around the world to our fertile waters. The White River is one of the few spots in the world where you can stand in one spot and catch the coveted grand slam! A yellowbelly, a striper, a rock, and a cutthroat can all be caught right here on the White River.
