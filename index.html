Welcome to Bassholes Fishing Guides


The fish are always biting in Beaver Lake, the White River, and the surrounding areas. Venture out on the water with one of our professional guides who have years of experience. Whether you're a fishing veteran or trying it out for the first time, you're sure to find the perfect spot in the area with our guides. Every day is a good day to fish. Licensed and insured. Offering guide trips for largemouth bass, smallmouth bass, spotted bass, stiper, hybrid, and white bass. We supply the cooler and ice, just bring your beverage of choice and a positive attitude and readiness to fish. Bass fishing in the Ozark region can be a great outdoor experience for you, your friends, and other guests. There is nothing more rewarding than putting the line in on a quiet lake somewhere, getting a nibble, and then hooking a record!
